About

A community-centered space for growth, encouragement, and connection.

Oakland Youth Aspire supports youth in Oakland through mentorship, healthy living, and life-skills development.

Our programs are centered on creating a safe and encouraging environment where young people can build confidence, strengthen communication, and grow within their community.

Programs

What we focus on

Mentorship

Supportive guidance and consistent relationships that help young people navigate challenges, build confidence, and feel seen within their community.

Healthy Living

Encouraging wellness, nutrition, and healthy habits that support long-term growth both inside and outside the program.

Life Skills

Building communication, responsibility, and everyday practical skills that prepare youth for school, relationships, and future opportunities.
