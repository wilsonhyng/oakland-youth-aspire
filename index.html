About

Oakland Youth Aspire supports youth in Oakland through mentorship, healthy living, and life-skills development.

Our programs create a safe, welcoming environment where young people can build confidence, learn practical skills, and develop meaningful relationships within their community.

Programs

Mentorship

Supportive relationships that help youth grow and navigate challenges.

Healthy Living

Nutrition, wellness, and habits that support long-term health.

Life Skills

Communication, responsibility, and everyday skills for independence.
